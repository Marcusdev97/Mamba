## Additional info:
1.  What are the future development plans for lifestyle-related amenities (e.g. size and timeline of a supermarket, whether there will be a café, restaurant, etc.)?
- The developer will manage the retail component to ensure a well-curated retail mix for Khaya Residences. With this objective, ME plans to introduce lifestyle-oriented retailers such as artisan cafés, wellness centers, groceries, convenience stores, and fitness centers.

2.  How will the noise from the nearby mosque affect the project, and what kind of sound insulation will be used in the building?
- The mosque opposite Khaya Residences only has chanting that lasts for about 5 minutes. Nevertheless, our project team will engage a consultant to conduct decibel measurements once the tower is completed, and we will explore the option of installing double-glass windows for the affected units if needed.

3.  Has the TNB office building already started leasing? What is the estimated occupancy rate? What type of companies are expected to move in?
- The TNB office, managed directly by TNB, has commenced its leasing activities. The first confirmed tenant will be moving in by December 2025, with secured occupancy reaching 15% as of September. Most of the tenants are multinational corporations (MNCs), given that the net lettable areas are relatively large—ranging from 8,000 sq. ft. to 14,000 sq. ft.—a size typically suited for big corporations. By the time Khaya is handed over at the end of 2028, the office tower is expected to achieve a healthy level of occupancy.


4.  What are the developer’s plans for the surrounding areas?
- The land size under our ownership is 3.8 acres, comprising Office, Retail, and Serviced Apartment components, which have been fully illustrated in our renderings. One of the key advantages of Khaya Residences is that the surrounding area is already fully developed, leaving minimal opportunity for any other future development.

5. Any covered walkway to mid valley?
There are covered walkway to Abdullah Hukum & then linked to KL Eco city before proceed to Gardens/Mid Valley. However, To Abdullah Hukum is around 400m, then to Mid Valley another 300-400m (approximately) (Refer propkini document: Link to KL Eco CIty & Mid Valley)
