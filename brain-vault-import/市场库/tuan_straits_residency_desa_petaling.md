## Sales Package
1.	Base Rebate
A total rebate of 10% of the Purchase Price shall be offered to the Buyer, structured as follows:
•	8% upon signing of the Sale and Purchase Agreement (“SPA”)
•	2% provided the SPA is signed within fourteen (14) days from the date of Notice of Signing.

2.	Additional Option
Buyers may select one (1) of the following options only:

a. An additional rebate of RM30,000; OR

i. 90% Loan with Additional RM30,000 Rebate
•	Where the Buyer obtains a 90% loan and opts for the additional RM30,000 rebate, the rebate shall be deducted and refunded at the final billing stage.

ii. Loan Less Than 90% with Additional RM30,000 Rebate
•	Where the Buyer’s approved loan amount is less than 90%, and the Buyer opts for the additional RM30,000 rebate, the rebate may be used to settle the loan differential sum.

iii. Cash Buyer
•	For cash buyers, the 10% rebate and the additional RM30,000 rebate shall be deducted at the final billing stage, provided the final billing amount is sufficient to cover the total rebate entitlement.

b. A furnishing package valued at RM30,000

•	Buyers who opt for the furnishing package shall not be entitled to any additional rebate, except for the standard 8% + 2% rebate and, if applicable, the Bumi additional RM1,500.
•	Once the furnishing package option letter is signed, the Buyer shall not be allowed to change or substitute the option under any circumstances.

3.	Bumi Buyer Additional Rebate
•	Bumi buyers are entitled to an additional RM1,500 rebate on top of the standard rebate.
•	This amount may be used to settle the loan differential sum or shall be deducted and refunded at the final billing stage.

4.      Agent Personal Incentive
•	Incentive of RM3,000 upon conversion, limited to the first 500 units only
•	Incentive claim form will be sent upon conversion.

5.      Bumi RM10,000 rebate
